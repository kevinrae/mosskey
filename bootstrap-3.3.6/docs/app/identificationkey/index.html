Multiple Entry Key for North American Mosses

A random-access, multiple entry key for the identification of the genera, and selected species, of North American mosses. Based primarily on A Perspective Oriented Guide For the Identification of the North American Moss Genera by Malcolm L. Sargent and Diane Lucas, 2006, et seq. Transformated from the dichotomous format by Stephen P. Rae and David Hutton, 2015-2016.
